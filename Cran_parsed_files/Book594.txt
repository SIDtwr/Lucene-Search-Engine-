./ wind tunnel techniques for the measurements of oscillatory derivatives .
./ bratt, j.b.
./ arc 22, 146, 1960
./ wind tunnel techniques for the measurements of oscillatory derivatives . this paper discusses the basic principles employed in techniques for the  measurement of oscillatory derivatives in wind tunnels, and gives some account of the associated instrumentation . the suitability of the various techniques for different test conditions is also discussed and brief reference is made to wind tunnel effects on the measurements .

./ buckled states of circular plates .
./ keller,h.b., keller,j.b. and reiss,e.l.
./ q. app. math. 20, 1962, 55.
./ buckled states of circular plates .   authors discuss the thin elastic circular disk of constant thickness subjected to a constant compressive thrust applied at its edge .  the analysis presented is based upon the nonlinear von karman equations of plate theory and is applied to disks with completely clamped and completely, simply supported edges .

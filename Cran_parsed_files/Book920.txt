./ supersonic flow over an inclined wing of zero aspect ratio .
./ stewartson, k.
./ proc. camb. phil. soc. v. 46, pt. 2, p307, april 1950 .
./ supersonic flow over an inclined wing of zero aspect ratio . an asymptotic expression is found for the lift distribution on a long, narrow, laminar wing, at incidence in a supersonic stream . the approximations of the linearized potential theory are used .
